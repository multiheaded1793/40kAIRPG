**CHARACTER SHEET: SHULIE**
# Background: Underhive Scum / Latent Wyrd
Wounds (Health): 10 / 10
Fate Points (Luck): 2 / 2 (Can be spent to re-roll a failed test or survive a lethal blow)
# CORE CHARACTERISTICS (Roll 1d100. To succeed, roll equal to or under these targets):
Weapon Skill (WS) - 25: You know how to stab, but you aren't a duelist.
Ballistic Skill (BS) - 30: You can point and shoot Granny's stubber well enough at close range.
Agility (Ag) - 45: Your best stat. You are fast, sneaky, and can squeeze through tight spots.
Toughness (T) - 30: You're scrawny, but you've survived Sump-water and bad rations.
Intelligence (Int) - 30: Not the sharpest, and never corralled into a parish school... but far from dull.
Perception (Per) - 35: You have good eyes and ears, trained to spot ambushes and marks.
Willpower (WP) - 35: You have a stubborn streak and latent, untamed psychic potential.
Fellowship (Fel) - 30: Moody and wary, but eager to gab when angling for something, or to calm the nerves.
# KEY SKILLS:
Stealth (Ag) +10: Target 55
Dodge (Ag) +0: Target 45
Awareness (Per) +10: Target 45
Deceive/Barter (Fel) +0: Target 30
# TALENTS & TRAITS:
Underhive Scum: +10 to tests navigating the Underhive or knowing gang/scum lore.
Latent Wyrd: You have unconscious psychic abilities. Under extreme stress, you can attempt to use "Granny's Luck" (Willpower test). Warning: Failing these tests can cause Warp phenomena.
# INVENTORY:
Granny's Six-Stubber (Ranged, 6 shots, decent stopping power, loud)
Folding Knife (Melee, concealable)
Ratskin Pouch & Chem-Mask (Prevents inhalation of toxic gases)
Empty flask of amasec
Granny's "Bad Luck Charm" (Focus item? Warp trinket? It resembles a tooth from afar, or a calcified eye when held up, and it burns when danger is near).
Ammo: 12 loose stubber rounds.
Outcast Rags
# APPEARANCE:
Scarcely above 20, lanky, corpse-pale, ragged, green-eyed, unkempt bushy hair.
