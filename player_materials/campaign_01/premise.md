## Campaign Background - Player Introduction
"I am lower hive scum on Necromunda, recently aged out of a street urchin gang, and a petty thief. When I try to steal from a bunch of offworlders at a notorious watering hole, and very nearly succeed – my grandma was rumored to be a witch... the last thing I would ever tell them, although I don't like the side-eye that the bald one is giving me – they reveal themselves to be an Inquisitorial cell delving into the underhive for something profane, and forcibly induct me as an acolyte to replace one they've lost. If I assist them faithfully without meeting my end at the point of some monstrosity's claws, or that of the Interrogator's bolt pistol, I may yet live to see the stars."
