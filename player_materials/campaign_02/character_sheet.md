# Character Sheet: Chiron
*A serpentine mutant pit fighter, driven by grief to unravel the legacy of his beloved witch, Briseis.*

## Characteristics (Base + Modified)
*   **Weapon Skill (WS):** 50 (Preternatural reflexes and unparalleled twin-blade mastery)
*   **Ballistic Skill (BS):** 25 (Rarely used firearms in the pits; prefers the intimacy of the kill)
*   **Strength (S):** 35 (Whip-cord, deceptive strength)
*   **Toughness (T):** 35 (Mesothermic biology makes him reliant on environmental warmth)
*   **Agility (Ag):** 55 (Slithering, unnatural speed)
*   **Intelligence (Int):** 30 (Quick-witted survivalist)
*   **Perception (Per):** 45 (Lidless eyes and heightened predatory senses)
*   **Willpower (WP):** 40 (Cold, obsessive focus)
*   **Fellowship (Fel):** 20 (Albinism, lidless gaze, and serpentine movements are deeply unnerving)
*   **Infamy:** 15 (A terrifying legend in the fighting pits)
*   **Corruption:** 20 (Warp-touched from birth + bearing Briseis's sorcerous amulet)

## Wounds & States
*   **Wounds:** 14/14
*   **Fatigue:** 0
*   **Status:** Healthy, cold-blooded. 

## Skills
*   **Acrobatics (Ag):** +20
*   **Awareness (Per):** +10 (Never blinks)
*   **Concealment (Ag):** +10 (Excels in the shadows)
*   **Dodge (Ag):** +20 (Serpentine evasions)
*   **Intimidate (S/Fel):** +10 (Uses his unnatural gaze and calm to unnerve)
*   **Parry (WS):** +20 (Master of the twin blades)
*   **Survival (Per):** +10 (Hive bottom dweller)

## Talents & Traits
*   **Mutant (Serpentine & Albino):** +10 Agility (already factored in), +10 Perception (already factored in), -10 Fellowship. Lidless eyes provide immunity to surprise from visual obscuration, but he is highly sensitive to bright light.
*   **Mesothermic Biology:** Chiron's metabolism is dependent on external heat. Prolonged exposure to cold incurs stacking Fatigue and penalties to Agility. 
*   **Two-Weapon Wielder (Melee):** Can attack with both blades in the same turn with minimal penalty.
*   **Lightning Reflexes:** Adds Agility Bonus to Initiative rolls.
*   **Step Aside:** Can attempt an additional Dodge or Parry each round.

## Equipment
*   **Briseis' Sorcerous Amulet:** A memento from his perished beloved. It hums with cold Warp energy. Its true purpose and power are currently unknown, though it occasionally whispers in Briseis' voice or pulses when near the Ruinous Powers.
*   **Twin Pit-Blades:** (Melee; 1d10+4 Rending; Balanced, Razor-Sharp). Elegantly cruel swords forged from starship hull plating, designed for flaying and dismemberment.
*   **Synskin Weave:** (Armor Value: 2, All Locations). A tight, dark, thermal-retaining body glove that helps regulate his mesothermic temperature and aids in stealth.

## Ambitions & Bonds
*   **Avenge Briseis:** Tear down the local Arbites precinct and the spires that ordered the crackdown on her uprising.
*   **Unravel the Agenda:** Discover what Briseis was truly planning with the Alpha Legionnaire.
*   **Fealty to the Giants:** Prove his worth to the Traitor Legionnaire and his strange associates to secure his own ascension.
