# Campaign 02: Black Crusade

## The Premise
You are Chiron, a mutant pit fighter who spent a lifetime bleeding for the entertainment of the privileged in the roaring, smog-choked bowels of a decaying hive world. Your existence was defined by the flashing silver of your twin blades and the cold, lidless stare with which you dismantled your opponents. You are an albino, preternaturally agile, and serpentine in both form and temperament—a mesothermic killer who required the heat of the crowds and the blood to feel alive.

It was only the presence of your beloved, Briseis—a fierce, unsanctioned witch who saw the coiled potential in your soul—that gave your violence purpose. She had been secretly stoking a rebellion among the labor-ghosts and the mutant underclass, weaving arcane machinations to tear down the spire-lords who deemed you both abominations.

But the Imperium's retribution is swift and pitiless. In the opening hours of the uprising, an Arbites kill-team stormed her sanctuary. She fell.

You arrived too late to save her, your blades slick with enforcer blood. But as she drew her final breath, three figures emerged from the shadows. They had come to meet her. They had come to deal with a nascent warlord, but instead, they found you—a grief-stricken, coiled serpent kneeling over a dying witch.

The first was **Khaenon**, a towering Traitor Legionnaire, his corrupted ceramite bearing the shifting, scaled insignia of the Alpha Legion. The second was **Valerius Thorne**, an Apostate Adept, urbane and razor-sharp, exuding confident, demonic scholarship. The third was **Xeta-Mnemnis**, a corrupted Skitarius—a spider-like woman with an ever-expanding panoply of gruesome cybernetics, hungering for scrap and flesh alike.

Taking up Briseis' sorcerous amulet—a strange artifact thrumming with dangerous Warp energy—you swore a blood-oath to these nightmare figures. For their patronage and their power, you offered your deadly skills.

You aim to avenge Briseis, to unearth the grand agenda she had just begun to set in motion alongside these giants, and to carve your place among the dark stars, no matter the cost. You will fight the hypocrisy of the Imperium with every vile, transgressive tool at your disposal. They called you a monster. You will show them a god.
