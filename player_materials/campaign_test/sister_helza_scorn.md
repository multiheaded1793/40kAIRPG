## Talents
*   **Faithful (Imperial Cult):** Once per session you may add your Willpower Bonus in SL when you make a Test related to your faith.
*   **Frenzy:** Make a Challenging (+40) Willpower Test to enter a frenzied state. Your Strength Bonus increases by +1, you are immune to Frightened, and you must attempt to engage the nearest enemy in melee combat each turn. You remain frenzied until all enemies you can see are defeated, or until you receive the Stunned or Unconscious Condition.
*   **Icon Bearer:** When you lift a holy icon aloft it sends your companions' hearts soaring even higher. When holding a Holy Icon you can confer the benefit of the item to any ally that can see you.

## Appearance & Personality
Hailing from the shrine world Macharia, Helza was indoctrinated into the Imperial Creed from her first breath. Her early life was spent in rapturous dedication to the Emperor. She joined the Missionarus Galaxia to spread the Emperor's word, but encountered the horrors of heresy in the field. Rescued by Inquisitor Halikarn, she now serves the Ordo Hereticus.

Fiery, pious, and utterly determined. Resolute in the face of hardship. An implacable (if intimidating) presence — composed, intuitive, inspiring, and charismatic. Her Ministorum credentials give her sway with the devout and open doors that would be closed to others. Her short temper is her greatest liability.
