## Talents
*   **Air of Authority:** If you make a successful Presence or Rapport Test the first time you meet a group, you have +1 Influence with them until the end of the adventure.
*   **Data Delver:** You have Advantage on Tests made to find or retrieve information from a document or archive. Additionally, you read text in half the time it would normally take.
*   **Dealmaker:** When negotiating a price, you can make an Intuition (People) Test to determine most beneficial course. You can alter the price of products by an extra 10%.
*   **Overseer:** As an Action, you may make a Challenging (Leadership) Test to aid an ally. If you succeed, your target may use your Skill instead of their own for the next Test they make.

## Appearance & Personality
Wilkus trained as a scrivener on the Hive World Persepolis, a hub of Administratum activity since the Macharian Crusade. He developed an eye for fine detail and leveraged his competence to climb the bureaucratic ladder — quietly filing for greater power, a more pleasant standard of living, and always staying a few steps ahead of any accusation. He met Inquisitor Halikarn during an investigation and procured the requested data with far less delay than expected. Halikarn requested his transfer to Inquisitorial service; his superiors gladly obliged.

A tactful speaker backed by thorough Administratum knowledge. Wise in the ways of seeking hidden information. A competent battlefield advisor should his words fall short. Ordered, cunning, and always calculating the angle.
